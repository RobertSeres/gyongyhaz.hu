## 2025-05-15 - [Form and Modal Accessibility]
**Learning:** In static single-page applications, interactive elements like timed popups and form inputs often lack proper ARIA attributes and associated labels, hindering screen reader usability. Using visible labels instead of just placeholders significantly improves accessibility.
**Action:** Always verify that form inputs have associated <label> elements and that modals use role="dialog" with appropriate aria-labelledby and aria-modal attributes.

## 2025-05-15 - [Gallery Alt Text Consistency]
**Learning:** Descriptive alt text in the site's primary language (Hungarian) is crucial for an inclusive experience, especially in a visual-heavy gallery section.
**Action:** Ensure all gallery images have descriptive, localized alt text.
